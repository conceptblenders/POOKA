# CLAUDE.md — POOKA Design Paper repository

Working instructions for AI assistants operating in this repository.

## What this repository is

This repository holds the POOKA Design Paper, split into chapter files under `paper/`. The paper itself is written in English. Conversational replies to Maarten follow his global preference and remain in Dutch.

## Ground rules

- The chapter files under `paper/` are the source of truth.
- Follow the editing workflow in `CONTRIBUTING.md` and the conventions in `POOKA_STYLE_GUIDE.md`.
- Work chapter by chapter. Never rewrite the whole paper.
- Do not invent content and do not silently improve the text. Propose changes; let the author decide.
- Preserve terminology: one concept has exactly one definition. Keep `references/terminology.md` aligned.
- Record review activity in `reviews/`.
- `archive/POOKA_v0.1.md` is a frozen reference. Do not edit it.

## Orientation

Start from `README.md` for the repository layout and `paper/` for the paper itself.
